About Company

Established in 1997, Todi Securities Private Limited is a SEBI-registered stock broking firm and a proud Trading & Self-Clearing Member of NSE & BSE across both the Capital Markets and Equity Derivatives segments. Based in Kolkata, we have built a strong legacy as one of Eastern India's most trusted and growth-oriented stockbroking firms, offering seamless access to India's financial markets.

At Todi Securities, we are committed to financial inclusion, ensuring that investors — both seasoned and new — can navigate the markets with ease. Our vision is to empower clients with smart, data-driven, and insightful investment strategies, enabling them to make informed financial decisions with confidence.

With trust, transparency, and customer-centricity at our core, we provide a seamless trading experience backed by deep market expertise and cutting-edge technology. Whether it's wealth creation, strategic trading, or risk management, our services are designed to help clients achieve their financial goals efficiently.

Our mission remains clear — bridging the gap between traditional investing and modern financial solutions, making trading and investing simpler, smarter, and more accessible for all.

⚠️ Attention Investors

1) Stock brokers can accept securities as margins from clients only by way of pledge in the depository system w.e.f September 01, 2020. 2) Update your e-mail and phone number with your stock broker / depository participant and receive OTP directly from depository on your e-mail and/or mobile number to create pledge. 3) Check your securities / MF / bonds in the consolidated account statement issued by NSDL/CDSL every month.

"Prevent unauthorised transactions in your account. Update your mobile numbers/email IDs with your stock brokers. Receive information of your transactions directly from Exchange on your mobile/email at the end of the day. KYC is a one-time exercise while dealing in securities markets."

As a business we don't give stock tips, and have not authorized anyone to trade on behalf of others. If you find anyone claiming to be part of Todi Securities offering such services, please write to backoffice@todisecurities.com
